FoxLake Welcomes You!

Foxlake Adventures is Scotland’s first cable wakeboarding park along with the UK’s only ropes course constructed over water. We are located in the heart of East Lothian 25 miles east of Edinburgh, nestled in a beautiful woodland on the edge of the John Muir Way.

Whether you want to give wakeboarding a try, take on the challenge of FoxFall Rope Course, Zip from tree to tree above the forest floor on ZipTrail, explore our woodland trails on Off-Road Segways or just chill out on the lake side with a coffee from the award winning Boardside café, Foxlake will have something for you.
